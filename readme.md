✅ Step 1 — Log out of Google Account (in Chrome)

Open Chrome

Go to: https://accounts.google.com

Click your profile picture (top right corner)

Click Sign out (or Sign out of all accounts)

If multiple accounts are signed in:
→ Click Manage accounts on this device
→ Remove each one using Remove

✅ Step 2 — Log out of ChatGPT (important)

Go to ChatGPT

Click profile icon (bottom left or top right)

Click Log out

✅ Step 3 — Delete Chrome Browsing History

Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete
(opens Clear Browsing Data panel)

Set:

Time range → All time

Check these boxes:

✅ Browsing history

✅ Download history

✅ Cookies and other site data

✅ Cached images and files

✅ Passwords (if available — important in lab PCs)

✅ Autofill form data

Click Delete data

✅ Step 4 — Delete Cookies Only (extra safety check)

In Chrome address bar type:

chrome://settings/cookies


Click:
See all site data and permissions

Click:
Remove all

Confirm → Clear all

✅ Step 5 — Remove Google Account from Chrome Profile

If the Google account is connected to Chrome sync:

Click Chrome profile icon (top right corner of browser — not the Google one inside websites)

Click the gear ⚙️ next to profiles

Find the profile with Sonushree’s account

Click the three dots ⋮

Click Delete

Confirm → Delete

✅ Step 6 — Turn Off Sync (if still showing)

Go to:

chrome://settings/syncSetup


Click Turn off

✅ Step 7 — Close Chrome Completely

Close all Chrome windows.

✅ Extra Lab Safety Tip

On shared computers always:

Use Guest Mode or Incognito Mode

Never save passwords

Always log out Google + Chrome profile
